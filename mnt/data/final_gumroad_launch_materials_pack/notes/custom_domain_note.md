# Custom Domain / Subdomain Note

Current setup:

Customer websites use Cookie Mini Website Builder subdomains like:

customername.cookiesdigitalcreations.com

This means the company domain remains at the end of the website address.

To remove cookiesdigitalcreations.com from the address, the customer would need a custom domain, such as:

customerbusiness.com

Recommended future upgrade:

Add Custom Domain Support as a Premium or paid add-on feature.

Suggested pricing ideas:

- Premium includes custom domain setup support, customer must own/pay for their domain separately.
- Custom Domain Add-On: $15/month or one-time setup fee plus hosting subscription.
- Done-for-you domain connection fee: $50–$150 depending on support level.

Until custom domain support is added, no paid plan removes cookiesdigitalcreations.com from the subdomain.
