# Final Soft Launch Test Checklist

Before public launch, ask 1–3 testers to test:

## Builder
- Could they understand where to start?
- Could they choose a template?
- Could they enter website information?
- Could they save a draft?
- Could they reopen the draft?
- Could they publish?
- Was mobile usable?

## Checkout
- Did Starter checkout open without 404?
- Did Business checkout open without 404?
- Did Premium checkout open without 404?
- Did Extra Page checkout open without 404?
- Did Gumroad ask for website name/subdomain?

## Customer Dashboard
- Could they find the draft or website by email?
- Could they open the website?
- Could they edit the website?

## AI Video Studio
- Could they create a creative kit?
- Could allowed plans create real video?
- Did the video appear on the site?

## Admin
- Did admin PIN gate work?
- Did Archive/Hide work?
- Did subscription page load?

## Final note
Collect feedback before a full public launch.
