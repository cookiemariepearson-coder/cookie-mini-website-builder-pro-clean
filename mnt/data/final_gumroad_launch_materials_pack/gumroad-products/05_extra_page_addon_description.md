The Extra Page Add-On is for customers who already have a Cookie Mini Website Builder subscription and need more room on their website.

Use this add-on when you want to add another page such as:

- Menu
- Gallery
- Products
- Services
- Portfolio
- FAQ
- Testimonials
- Booking/contact information
- Extra business details

Important add-on notes:

- This is a monthly add-on subscription.
- Extra pages are $10/month per page.
- You must keep your main website subscription active.
- If your main website subscription or extra page add-on is canceled, refunded, disputed, or payment fails, extra page access may be paused or removed.
- Customer is responsible for all uploaded content and business information.

After purchase, return to Cookie Mini Website Builder and use the same website name/subdomain you are upgrading.
