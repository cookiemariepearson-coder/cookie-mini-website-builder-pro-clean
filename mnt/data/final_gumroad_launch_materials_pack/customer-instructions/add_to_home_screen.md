# Add to Home Screen Instructions

Customers can add Cookie Mini Website Builder or their website dashboard to their phone home screen.

## Android

1. Open the page in Chrome.
2. Tap the three-dot menu.
3. Tap Add to Home screen.
4. Confirm the name.
5. Tap Add.

## iPhone / iPad

1. Open the page in Safari.
2. Tap the Share button.
3. Tap Add to Home Screen.
4. Confirm the name.
5. Tap Add.
