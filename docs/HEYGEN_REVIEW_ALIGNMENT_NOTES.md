# HeyGen Policy Review Alignment Notes

This pack does not copy HeyGen's policies. It uses Cookie Mini Website Builder Pro-specific wording aligned with the categories that matter when the platform connects to AI video tools:

- third-party AI service processing
- input/output responsibility
- AI moderation/refusal
- usage caps and feature limits
- commercial use responsibility
- likeness, face, voice, and consent
- biometric/privacy awareness
- subscriptions, cancellations, refunds, and third-party limits
- no guaranteed results
- customer review before publication

Have a qualified legal professional review the final language before a full public launch.
