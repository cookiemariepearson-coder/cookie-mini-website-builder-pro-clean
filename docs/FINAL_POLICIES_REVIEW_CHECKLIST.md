2. Pricing page mentions subscriptions and cancellation policy.
3. Gumroad product pages match the plan limits.
4. Gumroad checkout asks for “Website name or subdomain you are upgrading.”
5. Customer guide links to policy hub.
6. Free and Starter users understand real AI video is not included unless manually approved.
7. Business and Premium AI video limits match admin settings.
8. Refund wording matches what you are willing to honor.
9. Website archive/pause wording matches admin controls.
10. Have a qualified legal professional review these before heavy public promotion.
